About Us

Founded on the principles of exceptional craftsmanship and unparalleled service, Tesoura de Prata represents the pinnacle of masculine grooming excellence. Our story began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where modern gentlemen could experience the timeless art of traditional barbering.

Every cut, every shave, every detail is executed with precision and passion. We believe that grooming is not just about appearance—it's about confidence, self-expression, and the ritual of self-care that every distinguished man deserves.

"Excellence is not a skill, it's an attitude we bring to every client, every day."
